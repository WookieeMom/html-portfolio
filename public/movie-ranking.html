Top 3 Movie Night Picks

Splendor in the Grass - 1961

A classic film with Warren Beatty and Natalie Wood, sure to plesae your tribe!

What to expect...

  • Action!
  • Romance!
  • Drama!
  • Heartbreak!
  • Redemption!

Pretty Woman - 1990

A young Richard Gere and Julia Roberts team up for an opposites attract love story. A rich business man and a prositute meet by chance in Hollywood. They form an unlikely alliance while hilarity and drama ensue.

A Beautiful Mind - 2001

Jennifer Connelley and Russel Crowe shine in this unique story of a genius in Mathematics (Crowe) on the brink of acclaim. The couple face challenges as this beautiful mind seems to unravel into hallucination and paranoia. A film that will have you questioning reality.
